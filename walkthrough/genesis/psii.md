## I. Story

1000 years ago, the heroine Alis defeated the evil King Lassic and freed the
Algo Star System of the tyrant and of evil. However, since then Algo has been
reliant on the super computer "Mother Brain", especially the planet of Motavia
(or as the game calls it, Mota). Motavia has been transferred from the desert
it once was to a green, flourishing planet like that of Palma (or Palm). But,
the people of Motavia have become weaker because they rely solely on Mother
Brain. However, evil starts to threaten Algo once again.

## III. Walkthrough

When you start Phantasy Star II, you'll be taken to a dream. Rolf will say
that he keeps having dreams of a girl being beaten by a demon. Anyway, you'll
figure that out later. Rolf awakens and walks over to the Central Tower in
Paseo, Mota (Motavia from the other PS games). The commander wants Rolf to
find something in the Bio-systems Lab. Rolf goes back home and is surprised
when a girl he found one day, Nei, won't let him go unless she goes. Rolf, not
having the time to argue, decides to let her go.

---

## a. Many, many fights

Paseo:

Now you have control of your characters. The first thing you'll want to do is
buy 2 Steel Bars for Nei at the Weaponry in town. Equip them on her then save
at the Data Memory in town. When you are ready, walk outside.

Mota:

For now, just stay around the area and don't bother going through the bridge
yet. Fight monsters around this area to build up some levels. If you run low
on HP, go to the Hospital in Paseo to heal. Fight around until Rolf is at
Level 3 and Nei is at Level 6 (Nei will level up about twice as fast as Rolf).
Once you have the levels, go back to Paseo:

Paseo:

Buy Rolf another Knife here and a Fiber Coat. Sell the Carbon Suit. Rest at
the Hospital and then save. Head back out.

Mota:

Now you should be strong enough to go through the bridge. Walk north from
Paseo, through the bridge, to the other side. Now, walk north a bit and then
before you reach the footbridge (smaller bridge that you don't enter), head
east behind the domes and then south. You should eventually come to a town
called Arima.

Arima:

Looks like someone totaled this town. There really isn't much you can do here,
but, now that you've been here, teleport or walk (I suggest walking to save
money) back to Paseo.

Paseo:

Keep in mind that whenever you visit a new town, a new person will be waiting
for you at Rolf's house, waiting to join. Go to Rolf's house and Rudolf
Steiner will be waiting there for you. Rudo is a powerhouse, and should be
kept at the front line. Reorganize and put Rudo in. He'll be able to help you
out a lot. Exit town.

Mota:

Now time to level up Rudo a bit. Fight around until he gets to Level 3. It
shouldn't be too hard to do. Once you have that set, go back into Paseo.

Paseo:

At this point in the game, Rudo is much stronger with bladed weapons. Buy him
a pair of Daggers and toss the Bowgun. Rest and save again, then head outside.

Mota:

Make your way back to Arima. Fight around that area until Rolf gets up to
Level 7. It may take a while, but the money you'll make along with the fact
that Rolf will now know Ryuka and Hinas, will make things easier. Enter Arima.

Arima:

Buy Rolf a Sword right now. Trust me on this, you will very much need the
added strength and defense of this weapon in the battles to come. Teleport or
walk to Paseo now.

Paseo:

Buy Rudo a Telepipe and Escapipe just to have in his hands. ALWAYS keep one
of each on Rudo, as he's more likely to survive than anyone else. Buy
everybody about 4 Monomates and 1 Antidote each. Heal and Save, then exit.

Mota:

Go back towards Arima. Now, head east a bit to the footbridge, then travel
northeast from there until you see a building. This is Shure. Enter it.

## b. Shure Thing

Note: Make a note that all the dungeons in this game are pretty lengthy and
will make you endure a long, agonizing journey each time. For the first level,
Shure is not sure thing. Though the monsters for the first part are ones
you have already seen, it's still not exactly the easiest thing. Just so you
know, all dungeon walkthroughs will have me telling you where you should be.
I will also give recommended levels for each dungeon. Also, don't be afraid to
exit the dungeon and come back. Sometimes you will need to do that just to
survive.

Shure - Recommended Level: 7
F1 Treasures: Monomate, 150 Meseta, 40 Meseta, Dynamite x2
F2 Treasures: Dimate
F3 Treasures: Headgear, 200 Meseta
F4 Treasures: Silver Ribbon, Letter, Small Key

Shure F1:

From the entrance, go left and follow up and around to the middle section (it
is really the only way you can go). There is a treasure with 40 Meseta, so
make sure you get that. Keep going left to the chute. Note the color of the
arrows around the chute. If it is yellow, you go up a floor, and if it is red,
you go down a floor. Take this chute up.

Shure F2:

Go down to the bottom and then left. When you see two walls and an opening
just above you, go up. Grab the treasure with the Dimate and take the chute
going up close to it.

Shure F3:

Go right a bit then up until you reach a treasure with a Head Gear. Give that
to Rolf and have him equip it. Go back down and take the chute you came out of.

Shure F2:

Go back down and then left and up. Follow to the top and then go right. Don't
go down the first path you see, but go right some more and take that path
going down. Take the chute going up.

Shure F3:

Go right a bit then down. Before you come to the place with the pits that take
you back to F2, go down a bit for a treasure chest with 200 Meseta. Go back up
and take the path to the left in between the pits. Go up and to the right.
Follow and take the chute going up

Shure F4:

It is always the last floor that has the toughest enemies, so be careful here.
From where you arrived, go up until you hit a wall, then go left to the wall,
then up. Grab the Silver Ribbon in the chest for Nei. Go back down and right.
When you're in an opening, go all the way down, then just follow the walls
going left. You'll go up and around, then back down. In the middle of a small
room at the end of this trek is a dead man. Search him to get the Small Key
and the Letter. Read the letter (which was intended for Darum, the guy blocking
the North Bridge) and the writer will say that he has Darum's daughter locked
in Nido Tower, and will only let her go for 50,000 Meseta. Well, you know
where to go now. But before you do, head back around and take the Chute back
down to F3.

Shure F3:

Go back right and around to the path between the pits to the chute back to
F2.

Shure F2:

Go left a bit, then down and right to the chute down to F1.

Shure F1:

Use the Small Key on the chest to open it up. It contains 150 Meseta. Go back
up the chute.

Shure F2:

Go up to the wall then left. Take the first path going down to the small room.
Take the chute going down.

Shure F1:

Again, use the Small Key on the chest here to get a Monomate. Take the chute
back up.

Shure F2:
Go back up then left and down. Go back to the room where you got the Dimate,
only this time take the chute going down.

Shure F1:

This chest is locked like the last two, so use the Small Key and take the
Dynamite inside (hehehe... boom!). Take the chute back up.

Shure F2:

Go down from the chute and then right. There should be a small room with two
chutes. Take the one going down.

Shure F1:

For the last time, use the Small Key and pick up the 2nd stick of Dynamite.
Now you are free to use Hinas to get out of here.

Mota:

Either walk back or use Ryuka, depending on how much life you have left in you.

Paseo:

Heal here and head to the Storage Room in the Central Tower. You won't need
the Small Key anymore, so store that in there since you can't sell it. Sell
any un-needed items and save up. Head to Arima.

Arima:

Now you'll really need a boost of power. Buy Rudo 2 Ceramic Knives. Yes, they
are VERY expensive, but you'll thank me later when you have the extra EXP to
help you out. If you don't have the money, go back to the area around Shure
and fight around until you do. It takes 5600 Meseta to get two of them. Make
SURE you do that. Head back to Paseo once you have it.

Paseo:

Sell the Daggers and anything else you don't need. Buy some more Monomates and
finally save up. Head outside.

Mota:

Now you have to head to Nido. Make your way back to Shure then go south from
there across a footbridge. Go southwest from there and you will soon reach
Nido Tower.

## c. Nido, Tower of Death

Nido - Recommended Level: 8
F2 Treasures: Dimate, 20 Meseta, 100 Meseta
F3 Treasures: 30 Meseta, Trimate, Teim

Nido F1:

Though this tower has many paths to take, many leading to dead-ends, it is
always good in this game to get every treasure in each dungeon. From the
entrance, go up to the door and use the Dynamite. The door will explode open,
allowing you to get in. Go up past the door and go right to the wall, go up
then left. Take the chute going up.

Nido F2:

Go up to get 100 Meseta from the chest. Go right and down until you find a
chute going up.

Nido F3:

The 3rd floor is FULL of nasty monsters. Be very careful around Blasters, as
they can drop you like a fly without even breaking a sweat. Anyway, go up a
bit then left to the wall. Go up to the wall, right a bit, up to the wall, and
left to a chest with a Trimate. Go back to the chute you came from.

Nido F2:

Go up and left back to that chute that you took before.

Nido F1:

From here, go left to the wall, then up to the wall. Go right a bit and then
up to the chute.

Nido F2:

Go down, left a bit, then down again. At the bottom wall, go left until you
see a chest with 20 Meseta. Go back up to the chute. Again, go down a bit, then
left all the way this time. Down, left, and down will take you to a chest with
a Dimate in it. From this chest, go up and left, down and left again. Go up
and there will be a chute on your left and right. Take the one to the right.

Nido F3:

Go right from the chute and then take the first path going down you come to.
Go right again and follow to a chest with 30 Meseta. Go back to the chute.

Nido F2:

Go left and take the chute down.

Nido F1:

Go right and take the 2nd path going down. To the right is a chute going up.

Nido F2:

Go down all the way and left all the way to the next chute.

Nido F3:

Now you will want to hurry. Go down from the chute then right to the wall. Go
up all the way and hug the upper wall around to the little area in the center.
There will stand a girl. This is Teim, and she asks to be taken to her father.
Now you are done with Nido. Now make your way out before the Blasters spot you.

## d. Oputa, Oputa, Oputa!

Mota:

Head to Paseo to heal, then go north from Paseo all the way to the coastline.
From there, enter the bridge to the left. This is the North Bridge.

North Bridge:

Darum guards this bridge and won't let anyone pass without giving him their
money. Teim comes out with her face covered. Darum doesn't recognize her and
kills her. After killing her, he realizes that it was his daughter, and then
will kill himself. A sad turn of events, but at least the bridge is now free.
Exit the other side.

Mota:

Head west past the footbridge then south. Near the bottom there is another
footbridge going east. Cross it and enter the nearby town of Oputa. Just enter
long enough to head back to Paseo.

Paseo:

Go to Rolf's house and you'll find Amy Sage knocking on your door. She offers
to help you with her healing abilities. Amy is very useful for the entire game,
much like Rudo is. Reorganize and add Amy to your party. Keep her in back as
she is a very weak fighter. Head back to Oputa.

Oputa:

Now that you're here, buy Nei 2 Ceramic Bars and Amy a 2nd Scalpel. Though the
Scalpels aren't great, Amy shouldn't be doing much attacking right now anyway.
Now buy Amy a Fiber Cape and a Fiber Gear. Don't worry about Rolf and Rudo
right now. Exit to Mota.

Mota:

Make your way back to Nido.

Nido:

Keep Amy on the defensive for a while. Nido is the best place to level Amy up
for now. Though it may take a while, level her up to level 9. This is when she
learns Sar, which will REALLY help you out a lot. However, don't go to the
top just yet. Let her reach around level 5 before taking her to the top floor.
The experience is better on the top, so it should make things go quicker. When
you are set, head to Paseo and heal there, then head to Oputa.

Oputa:

Now you can buy Rolf and Rudo a Fiber Gear. Also buy Nei a Fiber Vest. You'll
also want to buy Amy a pair of Ceramic Knives. Though her attack will still
be the weakest, at least she'll be able to put up a fight now. Save and exit
this town.

Mota:

Go south then west from Oputa. Cross the footbridge and continue west to the
next footbridge. Go southeast a bit to the last footbridge then you should see
the Bio-systems Lab. Enter it.

## e. Nothing like Biology

Bio-systems Lab - Recommended Level: 11
F2 Treasures: Star Mist, Antidote, Scalpel
F3 Treasures: Dynamite
B1 Treasures: Poison Shot, Antidote, Trimate, Recorder

Bio Lab F1:

Now you have to use your last piece of Dynamite. This level isn't exactly the
most complicated thing, but with many twists and turns, it can be annoying.
From the entrance, go left to the wall, the up to the next wall. Left again,
up again, then left again. Go up all the way then right to the end. Finally,
go up a bit, right, the up to the chute.

Bio Lab F2:

Grab the nearby treasure with the Antidote. Go down to the wall then hug the
wall going left. Go down, following the wall still and grab the treasure with
the Star Mist. Now go back to where you got the Antidote. Fall down the pit
to the far right.

Bio Lab F1:

Go down and take the chute going up.

Bio Lab F2:

Go right to the far wall and then down. To the left should be a chest with a
Scalpel in it. Grab it and go back to the chute.

Bio Lab F1:

Go right all the way then down all the way. Go left past the test-tube things,
go up a bit, then left to the next chute.

Bio Lab F2:

Just go up and follow to the right for another chute going up.

Bio Lab F3:

This can be quite a trip. Go left form the chute then down all the way. Go
right to the far wall, then down a bit. Go right, down, and right again and
there should be a treasure chest with the last stick of Dynamite. Now make
your way back to the chute, only this time, go up and left. Now go up to the
top, left, down, and left again. Take the 2nd path going down, then go left
again and go down. Head right to the end and then up. There should be a door
here. Blow it open with the Dynamite, the walk in and fall down the hole.

Bio Lab B1:

You'll now wind up in a small room on the bottom floor. The bottom floor is
VERY dangerous, especially with all the damage floors. For every step you take
on those glowing floors, everyone in the party will receive 1 HP of damage.
Go left from where you land, then up. Walk over the damage floors to the left
wall then up. The least you could take on those floors so far is 5 steps. Now
hug the lower wall and you'll totally avoid the next set of damage floors when
you go left. Go up and you'll see another set of damage floors to your right,
guarding a treasure with a Trimate inside it. Step on the lower one on the
right and then go down. Grab the Trimate and go back the same way. You should
only take 2 damage this time. Continue going up and to the right. Follow the
path (you will have to step on damage floors, but to minimize the damage, if
you see floors that are diagonal from each other, step on one then to a safe
floor. When you get to the right room, there is a computer in the middle.
Search it to get the Recorder. Now, you can either use Hinas to get out, or
walk your way out. If you walk, go back to the room where you landed. Make
your way to the bottom wall. Go right all the way to the end. Down and left
will give you a Poison Shot. Go back up and left all the way. There is a
treasure with an Antidote near the chute. Take the chute when you are done.

Bio Lab F1:

Just go down to exit.

Mota:

Make your way back to Oputa, then warp to Paseo.

Paseo:

Head to Rolf's house and another knock will come on the door. This time, from
a Biologist named Hugh Thompson. Personally, all I do with Hugh is take his
equipment and sell him. I NEVER use Hugh, as his skills have no use to me at
all, and I recommend not using him as well. Besides, he's just about as strong
as Amy is, only Amy is more useful.

Anyway, once you leave Hugh to stay at Rolf's house, head to the Central Tower.
You will immediately take the Recorder to the Commander. After you find out
what went wrong with the Bio-systems Lab, the Librarian will give you the
Key Tube. Exit town when you have it.

## f. Cities by the Dump

Mota:

I recommend walking. Gives you plenty of exercise. Make your way back to the
Bio-systems Lab, then go south to the bridge.

Western Bridge:

The door is locked and you're out of Dynamite. Well, use the Key Tube to open
the door, and exit the other side.

Mota:

Go southeast a bit until you see a bridge. Enter it and go north. Enter the
town of Zema nearby.

Zema:

There is some good equipment here to get. Get Rolf a Ceramic Sword and give
Rolf, Rudo, and Amy a Titanium Gear. Don't worry about weapons yet, as the
Ceramic weapons should be just fine. Exit down.

Mota:

Don't worry about going back to Paseo yet to get your new partner. For now,
let's make our way to the next town. From Zema go south past the footbridge
and then head west to the next footbridge. Now go west around the domes and
northeast to the footbridge. Now make your way to the nearby town of Kueri.

Kueri:

This is a great place to get some armor. Though it is expensive, building up
the money for it is always a good thing. Buy Rolf a Titanium Chest, Rudo a
Titanium Armor, and Amy a Titanium Cape. Again, if you don't have the money,
take and go outside and fight. You will get excellent experience here.

Mota:

When you are set, continue fighting around some more. Build up about 9,000
Meseta by fighting these tough monsters. Don't be afraid to use Rolf's
techniques here in these fights, as some of these monsters are strong. Just
survive and rest at the hospital in Kueri when you are hurt. Make your way
back to Zema when you are set.

This is absolutely tough. Dark Force can possess any of your members to prevent

Brain should be easier to kill than Dark Force.

crashed Gaila into Palm. Lutz warps the other 3 members of the party to Noah

## IX. Stealing

One of the unique abilities in this game is the ability to have Shir steal
from shops. The only problem is that her stealing is totally random, and when
she does steal, what she steals is also random.

To have her steal, put Shir into your party and go into any shop. Exit that
shop. If Shir has stolen something, she will be gone. To find her, go to Rolf's
home in Paseo and the stolen item will be in her inventory.

If she doesn't leave, however, keep doing that. It may take many attempts
before she will steal. So don't get discouraged if she doesn't steal the first
time.

She will only steal 4 types of items from each shop, and she tends to steal
more common items. Below is a list of what she can steal.

Odds: Weaponry: Armory: Tool Shop:
1 in 2 Dagger Silver Crown Trimate
1 in 4 Laser Shot Jewel Crown Hidapipe
1 in 5 Laser Knife Green Sleeves Star Mist
1 in 20 Fire Staff Shune Boots Moon Dew

There is also 1 very special item that Shir can steal only from the Central
Tower in Paseo. This item is the Visiphone, a very helpful item for this game.
Shir needs to be at level 10 though. To get it, enter the Central Tower in
Paseo and enter the Storage Room. If Shir is gone when you leave, head to
Rolf's home and she'll have the Visiphone. If she doesn't steal, just keep
trying.

Make a note here; NEVER take Shir to another town other than Paseo. The reason
being is because she can steal from any shop at any time. Imagine going to
Dezo with Shir, going to a shop in one of the towns, and then realizing that
she is gone. God only knows how she gets back to Mota, but you have to go ALL
the way back to get her.

## X. Tips

1. It is VERY important that you level up in this game. I don't know how many
   times I've had to tell people to level up before heading to a place like Nido
   or the Bio-systems Lab. A good way to make sure that you are at a good level is
   to buy the best equipment money can buy before going to the place you have to
   go to. In order to gain the money, you have to fight. By the time you get all
   the items you can afford, you'll be powered up enough to survive easily through
   your destination.

2. When you want to build up a specific character (usually Kain before heading
   to the Dams and Anna before heading to Dezo), just take that character with
   Rolf. You don't always have to take 4 characters with you, heck you don't have
   to take any if you don't want to. You just have to cancel once you reorganize
   at Rolf's home with the people you want.

3. The Visiphone is the single most important item that you can get in this
   game. It will save you from having to restart the level when you die by
   allowing you to save anywhere you want. Make sure that once you get Shir to
   level her up to level 10 and then steal one.

4. During the later levels, monsters and robots will start to do heavy damage
   on you. But, there is one trick that can be used to minimize the damage,
   though it could be lengthy, it will save you a LOT. Give the Snow Crown to
   Anna and the Storm Gear to Amy. Have Anna start the fight using the Snow Crown
   while Amy always uses the Storm Gear. Anna's speed will always start the fight
   with the Snow Crown while the Storm Gear casts GiZan on all enemies. Instead
   of having the monsters damage you for 60 HP a shot, the damage will be a LOT
   less with this method.
